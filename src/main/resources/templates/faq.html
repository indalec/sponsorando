Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sponsorando and our unique Altruistic Sponsoring model.

Sponsorando is a platform that connects sponsors with creators and social initiatives, fostering partnerships that create positive impact beyond just marketing exposure. Unlike traditional sponsorship, Sponsorando promotes Altruistic Sponsoring.

Altruistic Sponsoring is a new sponsorship model where the primary reward isn’t marketing exposure but meaningful impact. Instead of focusing on ROI like brand visibility or sales, sponsors support projects driven by shared values, emotional satisfaction, and social good. It’s about fostering real change through empathy, trust, and human connection. Sponsors receive updates on how their contributions transform lives, creating a deep sense of fulfillment. By prioritizing purpose over profit, Altruistic Sponsoring shifts the narrative—turning sponsorship into a force for genuine progress rather than just a business transaction. It’s sponsorship with heart, making a lasting difference.

Sponsors browse projects aligned with their values, contribute funds, and track the impact of their sponsorship in real time. Creators receive support to bring their ideas to life.

Anyone—from individuals to businesses and organizations—who wants to support meaningful projects can become a sponsor.

Projects range from artistic endeavors and educational programs to social causes and community initiatives.

Creators can start a campaign by signing up, submitting their project details, and sharing how sponsorship will make an impact.
